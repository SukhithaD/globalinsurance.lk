About GIBS

Global Insurance Brokers & Services (Pvt) Ltd was incorporated as a limited liability company on 10th July 2007. As an unbiased and leading insurance broker, we provide the best terms from registered insurance companies, guaranteeing you the best rate and coverage. Our team consists of experienced, efficient, and reliable insurance professionals who have been serving the industry for over 20 years.

Our knowledge and expertise differentiate our services and provide clients with solutions to meet their key insurance challenges. With us, you are never alone!
